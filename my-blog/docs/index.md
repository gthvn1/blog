# Xen & Storage

Welcome to **Xen Storage Insights**, a blog dedicated to exploring the intersection
of virtualization and storage in the context of Xen. Here, we'll dive into the technical
workings of how storage solutions integrate with the Xen hypervisor, examining many things
from virtual disks to snapshots and advanced storage technologies like VHD, QCOW formats
but also Tapdisk or the Storage Manager API (aka SMAPI).

Through detailed analysis and practical tutorials, we aim to enlight the complexities
behind storage management in virtualized environments, helping you understand how Xen
handles storage and how to optimize it for performance, reliability, and scalability.
Whether you're a seasoned sysadmin or just curious about the inner workings of Xen,
this blog is your go-to resource for in-depth knowledge and hands-on advice.
